About Us

At DurianBoy Jeff, we believe durians should be enjoyed the way nature intended, fresh, flavorful, and straight from the farm. That’s why we bring you the best durians, picked at their peak and delivered daily for maximum freshness.

We’re passionate about giving our customers a true farm-to-table experience, so every durian you taste is as authentic and delicious as it gets. With a wide selection of varieties to choose from, there’s always something new to discover, whether you’re a durian enthusiast or trying it for the first time.

Freshness. Variety. Quality. That’s what we stand for.

Durian Varieties

Our most popular selections. Prices vary by season. Contact us for today’s rate.

D197 • Musang King

Yellow, creamy flesh with a rich sweetness that finishes with a pleasant, subtle bitterness.

D200 • Black Thorn

Thick, creamy texture with a gentle wine-like (fermented) note and striking orange flesh.

D24 • XO

Classic D24 profile with a pronounced bitter edge and lightly fermented character.

D175 • Udang Merah (Red Prawn)

Fibreless, creamy flesh celebrated for its rich, sweet, fruity flavour. A northern Malaysian favourite.

D160 • Tekka (Musang Queen)

Golden, sticky flesh with a bold bitter profile elegantly balanced by a sweet aftertaste.

D163 • Horloh

Dry, sticky flesh with a slightly bitter, floral character. Bold and creamy.

Why Durian is the King of Fruits

Rich, Complex Flavour

Creamy textures, balanced sweetness and a delightful aroma. Each variety offers a distinct, satisfying taste.

Nutrient Powerhouse

Packed with vitamins, fiber, potassium and antioxidants. Durian is both indulgent and nourishing.

Cultural Pride

Revered across Southeast Asia, durian is celebrated as a seasonal delicacy that brings families and friends together.
